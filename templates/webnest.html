Web Nest Technology Club

Where Innovation Meets the Web — Building the Future of Technology

About Us

Web Nest is a creative technology club under the Nexus Forum. We aim to foster innovation and technical excellence among students by providing hands-on experience in web development, programming, and emerging technologies.

Our Vision

To empower tech enthusiasts to design, develop, and deploy solutions that shape tomorrow’s digital world.

Activities

  • Workshops on Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, etc.)
  • Hackathons and Coding Challenges
  • Tech Talks and Guest Sessions
  • Open Source and Collaborative Projects
  • AI, IoT, and Cloud Learning Series
